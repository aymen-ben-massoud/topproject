- Mobile: 375px
- Desktop: 1440px

- White: hsl(0, 0%, 100%)
- Black: hsl(0, 0%, 0%)
- Dark Gray: hsl(0, 0%, 55%)
- Very Dark Gray: hsl(0, 0%, 41%)
